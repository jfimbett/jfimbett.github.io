Overview

Why HVP?

1. Smart exposure to volatile U.S. companies

2. Low cost access to a long-short active strategy using high volatility stocks

3. Use at the core of your portfolio to get exposure to volatility

INVESTMENT OBJECTIVE

The AlphaGen HVP seeks to maximize expected returns in a Long-Short strategy by actively holding 50 high volatility stocks while seeking to maintain market neutrality, and a maximum concentration of 10%.
