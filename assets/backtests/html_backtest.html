Overview

Why TRP?

1. Invest in U.S. Public Companies in a market neutral portfolio

2. Maximize expected returns minimizing market risk

3. Use at the core of your portfolio as diversification

INVESTMENT OBJECTIVE

The AlphaGen TRP seeks to maximize expected returns in a Long-Short strategy by actively holding 50 high volatility stocks while seeking to maintain market neutrality, and a maximum concentration of 10%.
