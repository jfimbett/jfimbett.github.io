Overview

Why TRPL?

1. Invest in U.S. Public Companies

2. Maximize expected returns in a portfolio of 50 companies

3. Use at the core of your portfolio as diversification

INVESTMENT OBJECTIVE

The AlphaGen TRPL seeks to maximize expected returns in a Long strategy by actively holding 50 stocks, and a maximum concentration of 10%.
