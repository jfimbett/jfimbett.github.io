specialized. The course covered essential aspects of financial markets, including bond markets, equity
markets, financial mathematics, and derivatives markets, providing me with a strong foundational
knowledge. Beyond the coursework, it motivated me to deepen my knowledge through extensive
readings which helped me refine my performance in the subject.
In the same year, I also took the VBA Programming course, taught by Professor Frédéric Peltrault,
which built upon the basic introduction course we had in our second year. As there had not been many
substantive applications in second year, I found it rather superficial. However, by the third year, I was

mathematical reasoning with technical skills, not only to create and optimize the program but also to
consider user interface and presentation. This is the first time I've learned something directly applicable
to real-world work and the financial sector.
