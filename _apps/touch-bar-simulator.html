--- title: Touch Bar Simulator subtitle: Use the Touch Bar on any Mac description: Launch the Touch Bar simulator from anywhere without needing to have Xcode installed, whereas Apple requires you to launch it from inside Xcode. It also comes with a handy transparency slider, a screenshot button, and a service to toggle the Touch Bar in the Services menu or with a keyboard shortcut. (Open source) type: macOS date: 2017-04-16 repo_url: https://github.com/sindresorhus/touch-bar-simulator ---
